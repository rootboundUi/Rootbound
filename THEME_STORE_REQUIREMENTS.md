## Theme Details for Submission

**Theme Name**: Rootbound Premium

**Description**: 
A contemporary streetwear-focused Shopify Online Store 2.0 theme featuring modern design, powerful customization, and superior performance. Designed for premium fashion brands emphasizing authenticity and quality. Includes hero banners, featured collections, product galleries, customer testimonials, and full e-commerce functionality.

**Features**:
- Modern, premium aesthetic
- Fully responsive design
- Faceted collection filtering
- Predictive search
- Customer accounts
- Newsletter signup
- Testimonial section
- Image galleries
- Rich customization options

**Industry**: Fashion - Clothing & Accessories

**Industries Supported**:
- Fashion - Clothing & Accessories
- Fashion - Streetwear
- Fashion - Premium
- Lifestyle

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #000000 (Black)
- Secondary: #666666 (Gray)
- Accent: #E85D04 (Orange)
- Text: #1a1a1a (Near Black)
- Background: #f5f5f5 (Light Gray)
